ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Nelson David Daza. I moved from Long Island, New York to Southern California about 3.5 years ago in search of a new leaf on life, and to kick start my adventure into adulthood. I lived in LA for about 2 years then moved to Orange County about a year ago for a more peaceful life because the hecticness of the city wasn't my forte. I found the beauty of coding through my efforts in finding a new career path, as the pandemic made me reevaluate my sources of income.

Stability = King, and my gravitation towards all things technology helped me find coding as a new home for it. As much as I enjoyed the spontaniety of the freelancing life in LA, sometimes sacrifices must be made for the sake of comfort and stability, and in this way, I believe aiming towards a career as a developer will happily fill that niche.
